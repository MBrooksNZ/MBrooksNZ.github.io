Skills

"The whole is greater than the sum of the parts" ... These are some key skills I have accumulated, so far.

Cartography

I have a strong foundation of colour theory and typography alongside a long relationship with ESRI products. This provides for a wide range of cartographic opportunities when designing maps and spatial products.

Spatial Product Design

Creating the most appropriate product for a goal or challenge has a huge influence over the way I work. I take pride in producing high quality products, making the most of the tools we have available.

GIS Data Management

Carefully considered data management can have a big impact on the business. I aim to build the integrity of the organisations data through governance. We are responsible for the information that passes to others, understanding where that data has come from and if it can be trusted is key.

Consultation

Requests can often need a bit of unpacking, I try to understand how various parts of the organisation work. This is hugely valuable going into conversations with folk, who at the end of the day, are coming to you for help.

Workflow and Data Integration adviser

I love to look for new ways to help people. We can do so much with the tools we have. Connecting one table or system with another can open up a world of opportunties. I'm building my FME skillset with each new project.

ESRI Mapping Product Specialist

I've seen the Esri platform move from paper to web over the past 20 odd years. I've experience with geoprocessing, analytics and the desktop nuances of ArcGIS Pro to the administrative side of the various flavours of Portal (Enterprise and AGOL).
